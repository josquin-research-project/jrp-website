Browse by Composer

The Josquin Research Project presents music by both famous composers (e.g., Du Fay, Ockeghem, and Josquin) and lesser-known figures. Many works circulate anonymously; the JRP lists these by the sources in which they appear (e.g., Brussels 5557; Petrucci, Motetti A). Searching tips

You can use the word "or" in title searches to search for titles which may contain one or another word. For example, searching the title for "dei or deus" will find all titles which have either (or both) of those words in the title.
