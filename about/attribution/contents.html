Work attribution levels

Works in the JRP database are assigned an attribution level according to six categories. The following table shows the mapping to markers found after composer names' on the website. Josquin's works are assigned one of six attribution levels, while other composer's works are typically not assigned level 2, and merge levels 4 and 5.

Level Marker
(Josquin)		 Marker
(non-Josquin)		 Meaning Description
1 ✓ none Secure Very likely to be written by attributed composer.
2 (✓) none Slightly less secure Likely to be written by attributed composer.
3 (?) (?) Tricky Better than even odds work was written by attributed composer.
4 ? ? Questionable Less than even odds work was written by attributed composer.
5 ?? ? Doubtful unlikely to be written by attributed composer.
6 ??? ?? Very doubtful Very unlikely to be written by attributed composer.
